## Security Research

### CVE-2026-11855

**Unauthenticated Stored Cross-Site Scripting in Simple Membership**

- Severity: High
- CVSS: 8.8
- CWE: CWE-79
- Affected versions: Before 4.7.5
- Fixed version: 4.7.5
- Researcher: Duy Tran

The vulnerability involved an unauthenticated Stripe webhook path, persistent attacker-controlled metadata, and unsafe rendering inside an administrator-facing notice.
